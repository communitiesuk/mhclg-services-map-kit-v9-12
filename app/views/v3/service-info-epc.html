Overview of the service

Service description

An energy certificate is required when selling or letting most houses, flats or commercial properties. This service allows users to find accredited energy assessors to undertake energy assessments and issue certificates.

MHCLG's role in the service

MHCLG runs the register of assessors and the database of energy certificates, which are hosted on GOV.UK.

Policy

Policy objective

The policy aims to improve the energy efficiency of buildings and thereby contribute to the UK's target of net carbon neutrality by 2050. The UK’s building stock is a significant contributor to the country’s carbon footprint. Energy certificates are a valuable tool to support property owners to understand and improve the energy performance of their homes and commercial buildings.
